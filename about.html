About Us

Creating joy,comfort and happiness one batch at a time

Our baking story

Honeybunny Crummy began as a hearfelt gesture in 2020 , when founder Sindiswa Mchunu started baking from home, sharing her most delicious treats with friends, neighbors and local students in Soshanguve. From her freshly baked scones to her delightful death by chocolate cake.The main purpose is to bring people together through the comfort of freshley baked goodies. What started as a weekend of baking soon grew into a word-of-mouth favourite. Known for it's soft honey-glazed buns, butterly crumbs, and affordable treats,the bakery quickly became popular among local community. By 2022, HoneyBunny Bakery opened it's first small storefront keeping it's homemade charm while expanding it's menu.

Our Mission

Honeybunny Crummy bakery stays true to it's homegrown taste by means of using simple recipes, locally sourced quality ingredients and ton of love. Honeybunny Crummy Bakery aims to not just serve baked goods but to serve as a comforting space where people come for a little moment of happiness in their day.

Our vision

We aim to become a beloved neighborhood bakery known for spreading happiness through baking delightful treats. The bakery has a will to expand it's enterprise by means of accomodating cullinary students to get a feel of homegrown taste and experience the feel of the old good traditional simple baking.
